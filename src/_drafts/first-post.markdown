Yes, it's true. After many moons, months and years I have "officially" decided to start curating a blog of mine.

## Context

This monday (2025-12-01) in Portugal we celebrate the [Restauration of Independence](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Portuguese_Restoration_War) and it's a national holiday.

Obviously this beget my Blog, but how? You might ask.

I had reserved this weekend to migrate my old and rusty [Manjaro](https://manjaro.org/) install to a fresh [`archinstall`](https://wiki.archlinux.org/title/Archinstall) with [Omarchy](https://omarchy.org/) [^omarchy].

That took about 15 mins per machine [^lol], but because — obviously! —
I had already allocated my free time for this monstruous task for the entire next two weeks I was in dire need of something else to do!

And because I've been told — by at least one person (and I'm not counting my wife) — that I'm bearable enough when speaking about certain topics of my peculiar interest.

## Chronogram

So after:

1. Attending [Tim Bernardes](https://www.timbernardes.com/) show Saturday night — completely unrelated btw.

This blog was actually — maybe — created.
